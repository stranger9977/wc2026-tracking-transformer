The chemistry leaderboards

This is the answer to "who had the best chemistry at WC22." Two leaderboards: the offensive chemistry leaderboard ranks every same-team pair by joint offensive impact per 90 (JOI90), and the defensive chemistry leaderboard ranks them by joint defensive impact per 90 (JDI90). A third tab blends the two. Tap any column header to sort; the minutes filter cuts small-sample noise.

Distribution, visible pairs
One bar per pair, ranked by the active metric. Y-axis units match the table column.

JOI90 = joint offensive impact per 90 minutes shared (formal definition). JDI90 = joint defensive impact per 90 minutes shared (formal definition). Cross-Chemistry = α · JOI90norm + (1−α) · JDI90norm, with both metrics z-normalized inside the filtered sample. All built on VAEP — P(score) − P(concede) credit per action.
