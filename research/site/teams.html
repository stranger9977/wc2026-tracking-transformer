Team chemistry maps

The Top Pairs leaderboards rank pairs as numbers. This tab puts those same pairs on a pitch, one card per squad. The offensive map shades the pitch by JOI density (where each team's high-chemistry pairs combined to create threat); the defensive map shades by JDI (where they jointly suppressed expected opponent threat). Click any image to download the PNG.

JOI / JDI are defined on Methodology; the per-team optimal XI lives on Team Builder.
